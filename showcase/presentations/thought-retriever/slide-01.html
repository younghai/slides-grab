Thought-Retriever.

Don't just retrieve raw data — retrieve thoughts for memory-augmented agentic systems.

FENG · HAN · LIN · LIU · YOU / UIUC · MIT · CMU

PROBLEM
  • Standard RALMs: recall collapses beyond context window
  • Hierarchical retrievers: fixed structure, not query-adaptive
  • Long-context LLMs: costly, no persistence, no self-evolution
CORE IDEA
  • Persist LLM's reasoning as reusable thoughts
  • Retrieve over (external KB ∪ thought memory) — unlimited scale
  • Model-agnostic, lightweight plug-in
  • Self-evolves through user interactions
NEW SCALING LAW · DRIVEN BY INTERACTIONS, NOT PARAMETERS
